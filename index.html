About Us

EV Service aims to be the largest databse for EV charging stations so that all users can find an convinient and reliable EV station no matter where they are. We also plan on adding more functionalies such as adding your own station and trip planner so that you never have to worry about your EV vehicle getting discharged in the middle of your trip. Keep checking our website for more updates!
Our service enables customers to identify and go to the closest EV Charging Station by only creating an account on the internet. Currently, customers are able to locate the nearest stations, see the estimated trip time, and adequately plan their itinerary. Additionally, we want to improve the functionality of the website so that users can add charging stations that aren't already listed on the map. The first form will be the login page, where users must sign in to their accounts. Adding a report will follow the same format.
